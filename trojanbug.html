I am an architect. A pragmatic one. Completely immune to hype. One, who will tell you what your problem is, and not make you pay for solving the non-existent. Will fight, if needed, against you, if it is beneficial for you to be stopped. But I am also the one who tells you - "I do not have a clue", if I don't.

I am a manager. One who led teams through nightmarish projects, 70h workweeks and deadlines set two years back in time. One, who still have friends recruiting from those teams, who want and sometimes ask us to work together again. One, who has enemies, for it takes to make them the team to be built and the outcome delivered.

I am not modest. I know what I know. So I have no problem to admit what I don't know. I have a holistic approach, synthetic mind, work with patterns and not instances.
